About Me

I am currently a Ph.D student (since 2020) at spAtial Sensing and Computing Lab (ASC) in Xiamen University supervised by Prof. Ming Cheng. Before that, I spent a three-year M.S. study in Huaqiao University supervised by Profs. Jialin Peng. My interests focus on Medical Image Segmentation and Unsupervised Domain Adaptive Point Clouds Segmentation.
